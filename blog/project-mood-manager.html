Project Mood Manager

This is something I am currently working on. This idea is about creating a background application which will log your activities to check your mood.

An example can be, you generally type quickly when you are angry.

I was able to figure out around 35 factors which modify mood of person. I am going to use the same factors to motivate a person.

The benefits of this app is that people will be able to motivate themselves, which will reduce the stress level of people, decrease the chances of depression etc.

It will be good for big companies too as their employees will be working more as due to the good motivation level.
